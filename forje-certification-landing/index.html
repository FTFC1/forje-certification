Professional Retail Certification

Industry-recognised qualifications with clear career progression pathways

🎓 Career Progression Levels

Clear pathway from Associate → Senior Associate → Assistant Manager → Store Manager. Each level requires specific competencies and certification examinations.

📱 Self-Paced + Role-Play

Complete 5 monthly modules at your own pace, then demonstrate skills through manager-validated role-play sessions. Two training days per month for practical application.

🏆 Transferable Qualifications

Earn professional credentials recognised across Nigerian retail companies. Move between employers with verified skills and standardised competency levels.

⚡ Bi-Annual Certification

Every 6 months, complete industry-standard examinations to maintain and advance your certification level. Continuous learning keeps skills sharp and relevant.

📊 Performance Analytics

Track progress with detailed insights on skill development, completion rates, and readiness for promotion. Data-driven career advancement decisions.

🎯 Manager Validation

Local managers conduct monthly assessments with standardised evaluation criteria. Quarterly reviews by area managers ensure consistent quality across all locations.
